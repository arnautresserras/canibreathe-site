Know your pollen before you step outside.

Daily pollen and allergen forecasts, tailored to the plants that actually affect you — so you can plan your day with confidence.

Download on theApp Store Get it onGoogle Play

Private by design

canIbreathe works without an account and keeps your allergy profile on your device — never on our servers.

  • ✓No account needed
  • ✓Never accesses your GPS
  • ✓Your data stays on your device

Only the allergens that affect you

Pick from 27 pollens and spores the ones you actually react to. canIbreathe shows just those — not a wall of species you don't care about.

Know the best time to head out

The Plan tab finds the clearest air in your day with an hour-by-hour chart, and shows each allergen's typical season — so nothing catches you off guard.

Local forecasts, wherever you are

Real station data for Catalonia and the Balearic Islands — including the distance to your nearest station — with worldwide coverage through Open-Meteo. Track every place you spend time.

Set up in under a minute

  1. 1 Pick your language

    Seven to choose from — or just follow your device.

  2. 2 Choose your allergens

    Tick the pollens and spores you react to.

  3. 3 Set your location

    Search any city, or use a nearby PIA station.

And a few more things

  • 🔔
    Daily alerts

    A morning notification when your allergens cross the level you choose.

  • 💡
    Why levels are high

    See the reason behind today's level — rain on the way, dry and windy, or a seasonal peak.

  • 🌤️
    Weather & EU air quality

    Temperature, wind, precipitation and a 6-band AQI bar, right next to the forecast.

  • 📤
    Share the forecast

    Send today's levels to friends or family as an image or text.

  • 📶
    Works offline

    Your last forecast stays available, with a timestamp, when you lose connection.

  • 🌍
    Seven languages

    English, Català, Español, Français, Português, Italiano and Deutsch.

Trusted data

Forecasts built on open data from PIA · aerobiologia.cat for Catalonia and the Balearic Islands, and Open-Meteo worldwide.
