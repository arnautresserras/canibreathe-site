canIbreathe

Daily pollen and allergen forecasts tailored to the plants that affect you — so you can plan your day with confidence.

Get it on Google Play

What it does

🎯

Your allergens only

Pick the pollens and spores that affect you. The app shows only what matters to you, not everything at once.

📍

Local forecasts

Detailed station data for Catalonia and the Balearic Islands, plus global coverage powered by Open-Meteo.

🔔

Daily alerts

Get a morning notification when your tracked allergens reach the level you choose. No noise, just what you need.

🌍

Three languages

Available in English, Català, and Español. The app follows your device language automatically.
